## What is LumenFlow?

LumenFlow automatically converts green-screen videos into transparent WEBM files perfect for Unity game development!

## How to Use

1. **Upload** your green-screen video (MP4, MOV, or AVI)
2. **Adjust settings** if needed (chroma tolerance, quality, etc.)
3. **Wait** for processing (usually 30-60 seconds)
4. **Download** your transparent WEBM file!
